How Search Works Tonight

  • Audience members can browse and search songs. The room is not set to “known tracks only.”
  • Guests do not directly pick YouTube backings tonight. This room is using the host chooses the track model.
  • Behind the scenes, the room can resolve from known catalog and curated sources. The AAHF preset has YouTube and iTunes/song catalog enabled on the host side, with local-library audience search off.
  • If your song does not already have a clean known backing, it can still be requested. It may simply wait for the host to choose the best playable version.
  • Premium reactions and custom emoji are open in this room.
Best audience behavior: search for the exact song you want, submit once, and let the host/co-host team keep the night flowing.
