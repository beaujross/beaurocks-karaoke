# Social Mechanics Profiles

Status legend:

- `TBD`: pending interview confirmation
- `Draft`: partially defined
- `Locked`: accepted baseline

## Voting Framework (`voting_system`) - Draft

- Scope: Selfie Challenge, Doodle-oke, Bingo tile suggestions/approval.
- Intent: create fast crowd participation with low friction.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Encourage broad participation over strategic gatekeeping.
  - Avoid dogpiles and popularity pile-ons.
- Risks:
  - Vote brigading, unclear host override boundaries.

## Tight 15 Identity Loop (`tight15`) - Draft

- Scope: personal song identity list (aka "Type 15" in discussion).
- Intent: let singers express identity and reduce request friction.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Build recognition and performer confidence.
  - Encourage repeat participation with less search fatigue.
- Risks:
  - Stale sets and reduced discovery if over-weighted.

## Spotlight Mechanic (`spotlight`) - Draft

- Scope: host-highlighted user moments and callouts.
- Intent: create positive social focus moments.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Celebrate contributors and re-engage quieter users.
  - Keep spotlight temporary and positive.
- Risks:
  - Perceived favoritism, exclusion of non-spotlight users.

## Combo + Multiplier Energy Loop (`combo_multiplier`) - Draft

- Scope: reaction streaks, hype meter, score multiplier visuals.
- Intent: convert crowd energy into visible momentum.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Coordinate cheering and timing for shared hype spikes.
  - Make "everyone contributes" feel tangible.
- Risks:
  - Over-indexing on spam reactions or pay-to-win optics.

## Queue Governance (`queue_rules`) - Draft

- Scope: queue limits, rotation mode, first-time boost.
- Intent: keep fairness and flow during peak room load.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Encourage turn-taking and reduce line-cut frustration.
  - Preserve trust in queue order.
- Risks:
  - Rule complexity or hidden priority behavior.

## Reactions + Points Economy (`reactions_points`) - Draft

- Scope: paid/free reactions, points gain/spend, bonuses.
- Intent: give constant lightweight participation actions.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Reward cheering and room contribution.
  - Keep points meaningful but not oppressive.
- Risks:
  - Grind fatigue, economy inflation, unfair perceived advantage.

## Chat + DM + VIP Social Layer (`chat_dm_vip`) - Draft

- Scope: room chat, host DMs, VIP-only chat, slow mode.
- Intent: maintain social connection without disrupting stage flow.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Support quick coordination and playful commentary.
  - Protect safety with moderation controls.
- Risks:
  - Spam, harassment, fragmented conversation states.

## Tips + Reward Conversion (`tips_rewards`) - Draft

- Scope: tip links/QR, tip crates, points-per-dollar conversion.
- Intent: support monetization while reinforcing fun.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Encourage appreciation and room support.
  - Keep tipping celebratory, not coercive.
- Risks:
  - Pay-to-priority perception or inequity pressure.

## Ready Check (`ready_check`) - Draft

- Scope: time-boxed readiness pulse with optional rewards.
- Intent: synchronize audience between segments.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Create shared cadence before transitions.
  - Re-engage lurkers quickly.
- Risks:
  - Overuse causing interaction fatigue.

## Vibe Multiplayer Modes (`vibe_multiplayer`) - Draft

- Scope: Guitar, Strobe, Storm, Banger, Ballad energy overlays.
- Intent: periodic synchronized crowd mini-events.
- Intended social behavior:
  - Shared spectacle and rhythm participation.
  - Variety without breaking core karaoke flow.
- Risks:
  - Accessibility/sensory overload and mode overrun.
